# Niche Finder Scoring Matrix

> **Notion-Ready Template** — Copy this into Notion as a database, or use it directly as a markdown worksheet.

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Brainstorm 10-20 potential niches** using the ideas from Module 2
2. **Score each niche from 1-5** across the five dimensions below
3. **Apply the weighted formula** to calculate a total score
4. **Rank niches by total score** — your top 3 are worth validating
5. **Validate your #1 pick** using the 48-hour validation framework in Module 2

**Rule of thumb:** Any niche scoring **18+ out of 25** is worth pursuing. Below 14, skip it.

---

## Scoring Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | What You're Evaluating | Score Guide |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|-------------|
| **Pain Level** | 1.3x | How urgent and painful is the problem? | 1 = Nice-to-have · 3 = Annoying · 5 = Hair-on-fire |
| **Willingness to Pay** | 1.3x | Do they have budget AND history of paying for solutions? | 1 = Price-sensitive/no budget · 3 = Moderate spend · 5 = Already paying $1K+/mo for inferior solutions |
| **Competition** | 1.0x | How many AI operators target this niche? | 1 = Saturated (everyone's doing it) · 3 = Some players · 5 = Nobody's here |
| **Tech Feasibility** | 1.2x | Can you build a working solution in 1-2 weeks? | 1 = Requires deep R&D · 3 = Moderate complexity · 5 = Straightforward API + LLM |
| **Market Size** | 1.0x | Are there enough potential clients? | 1 = <100 businesses · 3 = 1,000-10,000 · 5 = 10,000+ businesses |

### Weighted Formula

```
Total = (Pain × 1.3) + (Pay × 1.3) + (Competition × 1.0) + (Tech × 1.2) + (Market × 1.0)
Max possible = 24.0
```

### Reading the Examples

- **Dental clinics** score high across the board — real pain (no-shows cost money), proven willingness to pay for scheduling tools, very few AI operators targeting them specifically, easy to build with SMS APIs + LLM, and 200K+ practices in the US alone.

- **AI content writing** has great tech feasibility and market size, but terrible competition (everyone's doing it) and low willingness to pay (clients expect $29/mo tools). The weighted score barely clears 18 — proceed with extreme caution.

- **Property management invoice processing** is a hidden gem — managers hate manual invoice categorization, companies already spend on property management software ($1-5K/mo), and almost nobody is building AI specifically for this vertical.

## After Scoring: Next Steps

1. **Pick your top-scoring niche** (highest weighted total)
2. **Run the 48-hour validation** from Module 2 — talk to 5 potential clients
3. **If validation confirms demand**, move to Module 3's 72-hour build sprint
4. **If validation fails**, move to your #2 niche and repeat

Don't overthink the scoring. Directional accuracy matters more than precision. The validation step is where you get real answers.
